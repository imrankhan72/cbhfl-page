Products Offering by CBHFL Housing Loans: To meet all your housing needs, CBHFL offers loans for purchase of house, construction of house, plot purchase , construction extension of house renovation and super finishing work. Non-Housing Loans: cbhfl offers loans to meet your business requirements like to purchase a shop or commercial plots. cbhfl also offers loans to meet your personal requirements like marriage education etc. by mortgaging your residential or commercial properties. Deposit Schemes: Investment scheme wherein you invest an amount for a fixed period and a predetermined interest rate. The period of investment or tenure can range from 1 year upto 10 years. The interest offered depends on the duration of the investment.
